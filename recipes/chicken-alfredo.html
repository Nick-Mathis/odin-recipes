Chicken Alfredo

Description

This easy Chicken Alfredo is the perfect go to dinner when you're short on time but still want something warm and satisfying. Using a jar of Alfredo sauce and pre-cooked chicken keeps things simple, while a handful of veggies and mushrooms round it out into a full meal. With just 5 minutes of prep and a quick cook on the stove, you'll have a creamy, comforting dish on the table in under 30 minutes. Great for busy weeknights or whenever you are craving something hearty without all the hassle.

Prep Time: 5 Minutes Cook Time: 15 Minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound uncooked fettuccine or linguine noodles
  • 2 boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cubed
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar Alfredo-style pasta sauce
  • 1 (10 ounce) package frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 (4.5 ounce) can sliced mushrooms
  • 1/3 cup milk

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Cook 1 pound fettuccine at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain well.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, place cubed cooked chicken, 1 jar Alfredo sauce, frozen vegetables, can of mushrooms, and 1/3 cup milk in a large saucepan over medium-low heat. Cook and stir until chicken is heated through and vegetables are tender.
  3. Serve warm Chicken and Alfredo sauce over cooked noodles.
